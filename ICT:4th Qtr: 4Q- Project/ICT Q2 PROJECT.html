Boracay

- Boracay, one of the top Islands in the Philippines, is popular for its beaches and blessed with a long stretch of powdery white sand, crystal clear and azure waters, and a stunning sunset.

  • Most iconic islands in the Philippines and has won multiple awards in previous years.
  • One of the most preserved islands in the Philippines to protect it from climate change, sea pollution, and plastic pollution

El Nido

- El Nido is one of the most popular tropical paradises on Palawan Island.

  • Located on the northernmost tip of Palawan
  • Abundant in stunning white sandy beaches and incredible lagoons waiting for you to explore.

Press here for more information on El Nido!


Baguio

- If you want to enjoy outdoor activities in the Philippines during the summer season without the sweltering heat, then Baguio is the place for you.

  • Dubbed as the "City of Pines" and is famous for its green park spaces, hillsides teeming with gigantic pine trees, and other North Luzon tourist spots.
  • The coldest spot in the Philippines

Press here for more information on Baguio!


Vigan

- One of the oldest towns in the Philippines, Vigan is a Spanish Colonial fairy tale of dark-wood mansions, cobblestone streets and clattering kalesa (horse-drawn carriages).

  • Finest surviving example of a Spanish Colonial town in Asia and a Unesco World Heritage site.
  • Home to many historic landmarks and is seen as one of the prime centers of the blend of Spanish and Filipino culture

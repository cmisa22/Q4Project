This Edition's Feature Queen: Marina Summers

Marina Summers is a fierce tropical queen known for her looks, hot body, exceptional dancing, and all-around performances. Her drag name stems from her love for the beach. Marina left her bucolic hometown of Nueva Vizcaya and moved to Manila to create content for one of the biggest film companies in the country.

She began performing as a drag queen in 2019 and recently released her debut single "I Have Arrived" on Spotify and iTunes. She performs regularly at Nectar Nightclub and is another one of the founders of Drag Playhouse PH.

Marina Summers hosts The Stop Over, an unofficial recap series for Drag Race Philippines season 2 inspired by the show The Pit Stop. The show airs on her YouTube channel, with guests for each episode being her fellow contestants from season 1, as well as other Filipino drag queens who have competed in a televised drag competition.

Upon her recent participation in internationally acclaimed show, "Drag Race: UK vs The World", Marina has made it her mission to put Philippines on the map.

As the lone representative from Asia competing at RuPaul's Drag Race UK vs the World Season 2, Marina carries with her the pride of being Pinoy: "Sobrang sarap sa feeling na pumunta ako doon hindi lang as a queen person but as a Filipino."

So far, she has been raking in favorable feedback from fellow drag artists and enthusiasts of the drag scene.

With two episodes out now, Marina left a remarkable impression as the drag queen to earn the first golden RuPeter Badge for the season, after winning the Lip Sync for the World challenge.
